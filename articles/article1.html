A Meditation on the Art of Icle

Like a rubberband around your arm

Date:

2014/05/12

Author:

Craig, The Bent-Nosed Cricket

At times, you want to snap it really hard. At times, you don't want to snap it at all. All of the time, you regret wearing a rubberband around your arm. Why? Because it is a dumb thing for one to wear around one's arm. So don't do it!
